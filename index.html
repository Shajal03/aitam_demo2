shipyard

A shipyard is a facility where ships are built and repaired, and may also be called a dockyard or boatyard. Shipyards typically have the following features: Building berths: A limited number of berths that slope down to the waterway Working areas: Large areas adjacent to the berths Drydocks: Deep drydocks are often used for very large ships

introduction

A ship is a large vessel that travels the world's oceans and other navigable waterways, carrying cargo or passengers, or in support of specialized missions, such as defense, research and fishing. Ships are generally distinguished from boats, based on size, shape, load capacity and purpose.
